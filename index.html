Current work

The nearest galaxies as detailed laboratories

My research uses spatially resolved, multi-wavelength observations to understand the feeding and feedback of supermassive black holes and their connection to galaxy evolution.

M31—our nearest massive spiral neighbor—is a central laboratory in my work, alongside M81 and other nearby low-luminosity active galactic nuclei.
