About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Astronomical Observatory of Japan (NAOJ), focusing on star formation, the interstellar medium, and multiwavelength observations of nearby galaxies. I previously studied black hole accretion and AGN feedback.

Research Interests

  • Star formation rate tracers (GALEX, WISE, ALMA)
  • ISM chemistry and molecular line diagnostics
  • Radio to X-ray AGN feedback
