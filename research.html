Research Interests

  • AGN feeding and feedback
  • Nearby galaxies, in particular, M31 (and local group galaxies), M81, and other nearby low-luminosity AGNs and LINERs
  • Multiphase interstellar medium (molecular gas, atomic gas, ionized gas, dust)
  • Photoionization modeling (CLOUDY, MAPPINGS, etc.)
  • Galaxy strucutre (formation and evolution of bars, spiral arms, etc)
  • Radio astronomy (JCMT, IRAM 30m, NOEMA, FAST, VLA, etc.)
  • Optical spectroscopy (esprcially IFU: CAHA/PPAK, CFHT/SITELLE, VLT/MUSE, etc.)
